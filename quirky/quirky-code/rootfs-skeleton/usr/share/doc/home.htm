DISTRO_NAME is a "quirky" Linux distribution. Enjoy!

Barry Kauler, the creator of Quirky, maintains a "news" page. Read about the background of Quirky, current work, and plans for the future:

http://bkhome.org/news/tag_quirky.html


Quirky originally forked from Puppy Linux, and has since gone off on his own quirky way. However, the applications, utilities and user-interface remain essentially the same. Therefore most of the discussion at the Puppy Linux Forum is relevant. The Forum has a very broad agenda and embraces all Puppy-like distros, so go there and join in:

http://murga-linux.com/puppy/
Exciting Internet links, but don't forget that right here in Quirky there is a lot of documentation. Click the icon at left, or go to the 'Help' entry in the menu (bottom-left of screen).
